class Patro.GeometryEngine.Path.LinearSegment(path, position, radius)[source]¶

Bases: Patro.GeometryEngine.Path.PathPart

Bulge

Let P0, P1, P2 the vertices and R the bulge radius.

The deflection \(\theta = 2 \alpha\) at the corner is

\[D_1 \cdot D_0 = (P_2 - P_1) \cdot (P_1 - P_0) = \cos \theta\]

The bisector direction is

\[Bis = D_1 - D_0 = (P_2 - P_1) - (P_1 - P_0) = P_2 -2 P_1 + P_0\]

Bulge Center is

\[C = P_1 + Bis \times \frac{R}{\sin \alpha}\]

Extremities are

prime P_1 = P_1 - d_0 times frac{R}{tan alpha} prime P_1 = P_1 + d_1 times frac{R}{tan alpha}
